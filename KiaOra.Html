The concept of well-being encopasses the Physical,Mental,Spiritual and Social dimensions of health.

Physical: The physical body.It's growth. Development.Ability to move. Ways of carng for it.

Mental:Coherent thinking processes. Ackowledging and exspressing thought and feelings. Respondoing constructively.

Spiritual:The values and beliefs that determine the way people live. The search of meaning and purpose in life. Personal identity. Self-awareness

Social:Family realtionships.Friendships. Interpersonal realtionships. Feeling of belonging. Compassion, caring. Social support
